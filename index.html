02 — About Me

A Philosophy of Place

Currently shaping my design voice at the Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD), my work is guided by the belief that a home is a feeling, not just a place. My focus is on creating environments that are both timeless and deeply personal, characterized by a masterful use of natural light, organic textures, and a curated selection of objects that tell a story.

My design process is rooted in the belief that the most successful spaces reflect the inhabitants' personalities and support their daily lives. This philosophy ensures every detail is thoughtfully considered and beautifully executed.
