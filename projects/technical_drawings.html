A Vision of Calm

These two furniture pieces, The Foyer Friend and The Haven, showcase the technical drawing skills gained through classes, professor guidance, and practice.

The Foyer Friend is a combination of a coat rack, cabinet, drawers, and seating bench that produces a system to help save space within the foyer or entry space of a home. This system helps provide hidden storage to keep the space clean, as well as protect it from the dust and debris of everyday life. The first sheet has the title, description, and orthographic views of the product, while the second sheet is the product itself. The blue graphite lines are guidelines that laid out proportions, perspective, and symmetry within the design and the black inked lines are visible edges of the hard surface.

The Haven is a redesigned, multi-functional restaurant booth that can be used in high-end settings to create a cozy environment that fosters socialization. The concept of this booth diminishes the worry for high-noise levels within the space, as the velvet backing absorbs sound, and the half-enclosed space does not allow large amounts of sound to escape. The booth includes features such as lighting for ambience, bookshelves for decorations, cabinets for storage, and a large seating area to provide comfort. The backing is velvet, the seats are leather, and the trim and table are wood. The top clear print sheet has the shading and coloring on the booth, while the bottom layer has blue graphite lines that shows proportions and perspective, as well as black ink lines that shows visible edges, orthographic views, description, title, and ornamental details.
