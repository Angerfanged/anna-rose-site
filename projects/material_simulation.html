A Vision of Calm

Each of these materials were rendered with markers then layered color pencils on top. This was a very fun project to do and taught me a lot about markers and their properties, as well as colored pencils and how they add depth and detail to each rendering. By doing this project, I learned how each material can be created to help me apply it to full interior renderings in future projects. I believe this is a strong skill to have as a designer to communicate ideas efficiently and create a unique style that can resonate with clients on a personal level.
