A Vision of Calm

The key goal for the artists' retreat cabin was to create a serene rural retreat that harbors creative focus. Located in Broken Bow, Oklahoma, this spot removes the artist from noise and responsibilities, allowing them to focus on their art while being inspired by nature in a way that the city does not allow. Broken Bow has a unique combination of deep forests, calm lakes, and an atmosphere that creates a sanctuary environment that permits the artist to be alone with their thoughts. Depending on the time of year, this cabin has changing scenery that is perfect to come year-round and experience all four seasons.

This residence is a one-bed, one and a half bath dwelling that has office/studio space and balcony on the secondary floor, and an open concept primary floor with a kitchen, formal dining, and living room. This design offers ample outdoor space that allows for connection with nature and adds to the retreat-like aesthetic. Focusing on texture varieties such as woods, stones, soft fabrics, and neutral colors allows the cabin to create a sense of warmth and coziness to encourage inspirations. Ample amounts of seating creates a variety of choices throughout the day and allows for other artists for families to visit for day trips.
